## Optimization Stuff
Some obligatory geometry review (don't forget your formulae, dummy)

#### Right Circular Cylinder
$r=$radius, $h =$ height
Volume: $V = \pi r^2h$
Surface area = $S =2\pi rh+ 2\pi r^2$   

#### Rectangular Solid
$l$ = length, $w=$width, $h=$height
Volume = $lwh$
Surface area $S = 2lw+2lh+2wh$
