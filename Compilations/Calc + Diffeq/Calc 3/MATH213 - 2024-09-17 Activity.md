Find the tangent plane to the surface

$$yz = \ln(x+z)$$ at $P(0,0,1)$

$$0 = \ln(x+z) -yz$$
$$f_{x}= \frac{1}{x+z} => f_{x}(0,0,1)= \frac{1}{1}= 1$$
$$f_{y}= -z => f_{y}(0,0,1) = -1$$
$$f_{z}= \frac{1}{x+z}-y=> f_{z}(0,0,1) = \frac{1}{0+1 }-0=1$$

$$0 = 1(x-0) - 1(y-0) + (1)(z-1)$$
$$0 = x -y +z-1$$

$$1 = x -y + z$$
